 E-Commerce Data Analysis Portfolio

Welcome to my E-Commerce Data Analysis Portfolio 
This repository contains datasets, Excel files, and Power BI dashboards demonstrating skills in data analysis, business analytics, and reporting .

Project Overview

 1. Sales Data Analysis (Kyrgyzstan E-Commerce)
- Data:Sales records for products in various categories (Electronics, Home, Beauty) from January 2025 to February 2025.
- Columns include:
  - `Date` – Transaction date
  - `Product_Category` – Product category
  - `Product_Name` – Product title
  - `Price` – Product price (KGS)
  - `Quantity_Sold` – Units sold
  - `Cost` – Cost of products
  - `Region` – City/Region of sale
  - `Revenue` – Total revenue
  - `Profit` – Profit per transaction

- Insights gained:
  - Top-selling products by category
  - Profit and revenue analysis
  - Regional sales trends
  - Promotion and discount impact on sales

2. Promotional Product Analysis (Wildberries)
- Data: Products with promotion information and stock levels.
- Columns include:
  - `Product Already in Promotion` – Indicates if product participates in promotion
  - `Brand` – Product brand
  - `Category` – Product category
  - `Product Type` – Type of product
  - `Product Name` – Product name
  - `Planned Promo Price` – Price during promotion
  - `Current Retail Price` – Regular price
  - `Current Site Discount (%)` – Discount applied
  - `Uploaded Promo Discount (%)` – Planned uploaded discount

- Insights gained:
  - Analysis of promotions and discount effectiveness
  - Inventory and turnover analysis
  - Pricing optimization recommendations

3.Invoice and Financial Analysis
- Data: Invoice-level transaction and balance records for a seller in Kyrgyzstan.
- Columns include:
  - `Invoice ID`, `Seller`, `Start Date`, `End Date`, `Payment Date`
  - `Total Amount`, `Net Amount`, `Fees`, `Balance`, `Currency`
- Insights gained:
  - Cash flow analysis
  - Net revenue and profit calculation
  - Payment and balance tracking
